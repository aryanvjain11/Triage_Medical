ESI Score Engine
Five Levels. Zero Guesswork.

The AI cross-references live vital telemetry with symptom analysis to assign the correct Emergency Severity Index classification instantly.

1 Critical

SpO₂ < 90%, Systolic < 85 mmHg, HR > 130 bpm, or cardiac/respiratory arrest

2 High Risk

Acute severe pain, stroke signs, hemodynamic instability, SpO₂ 90–94%

3 Urgent

Stable vitals with complex symptoms requiring multi-resource intervention

4 Less Urgent

Single-resource management needed, not at risk of immediate deterioration

5 Non-Urgent

Chronic conditions or minor complaints suitable for outpatient routing

Platform Capabilities
Built for the
Emergency Department

Every feature engineered for speed, accuracy, and the chaos of real emergency medicine.

🌐
Multilingual Symptom Translation

Accepts patient input in English, Spanish, Hindi, French, and Mandarin. Automatically translates to clinical English for medical record accuracy — no interpreter needed.

🫀
Real-Time Vital Telemetry Analysis

Inputs HR, BP, SpO₂, and Temperature. Auto-calculates Mean Arterial Pressure (MAP) and Shock Index to flag hemodynamic instability before symptoms escalate.

🤖
Multi-Model AI Router

Smart hub routes to Groq (Llama 3.3 70B), OpenAI (GPT-4o), or OpenRouter (Claude, Qwen) based on configuration — with automatic fallback if a provider is unavailable.

📋
Live Priority Queue Management

Dynamically sorted triage queue sorted by ESI level. Physicians can override AI classifications, reassign urgency, and release patients to assigned beds in real time.

🎙️
Voice Input & Audio Output

Patients speak symptoms directly — voice recognition captures in the selected language. AI generates a native-language reassurance script read aloud via browser speech synthesis.

💬
Multilingual Waiting Room Assistant

An empathetic AI chatbot answers patient questions in their native language — wait times, amenities, procedures — escalating to clinical staff when dangerous symptoms are mentioned.

🚨
Emergency Pulse Alerts

Critical ESI Level 1 patients trigger animated pulse alerts on the dashboard. Visual hierarchy immediately communicates risk to nursing and physician staff at a glance.

💊
Allergy & Medication Context

Allergy and active medication data are embedded into every AI prompt — giving the model full pharmacological context to flag interactions and contraindication risk vectors.

🛡️
Offline Fallback Rules Engine

A local clinical decision engine activates when AI providers are unreachable. Pure algorithmic ESI scoring ensures zero downtime — even without an internet connection.
